Our story

A studio built on slow making

El Taller started in 2019 as a single wheel and a borrowed kiln in the back of a shared workspace on Carrer de la Perla. What began as a personal obsession became, gradually, a place where the neighbourhood came to make things with their hands.

We believe in the particular satisfaction of holding something you made yourself. Imperfect, tactile, yours.

The space

A proper Gràcia studio

200 square metres of sunlit workspace on the ground floor of a 1920s building. Six wheels, two kilns, a long communal table for hand-building, and a kitchen counter where the wine lives.

The studio smells of clay and coffee. It usually has music on — not too loud. You'll be home by dark.

The team

Who you'll work with

Marta Solà

Founder & Head Instructor

Marta trained at the Escola Massana in Barcelona and spent three years in a studio collective in Kyoto before returning to open El Taller. She teaches every session herself alongside a small team of instructors she trained. Expect direct feedback, a lot of patience, and very good playlists.
