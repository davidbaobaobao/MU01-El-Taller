Before you come

Common questions

Do I need any experience? +

Absolutely none. Our sessions are designed for complete beginners. Our instructors guide you through everything step by step — you just need to show up.

What should I wear? +

Something you don't mind getting clay on. We provide aprons but sleeves and anything light-coloured are at their own risk. Closed-toe shoes recommended.

When do I get my piece? +

Pieces are fired and glazed in the two weeks after your session. We'll email you when they're ready to collect from the studio.

Can I cancel or reschedule? +

Yes — up to 48 hours before your session for a full refund. Within 48 hours, we can reschedule you to a future date but cannot refund. Email us to sort it.
